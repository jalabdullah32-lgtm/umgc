Who am I?

My name is Jalal Abdullah. I am 23 year old senior, completing my bachelors degree at University of Maryland Global Campus. When I'm not in school, you'll find me either working or watching unproductive YouTube videos.

Why I started baking?

Before the cookies, I had dreams of becoming a professional soccer player. An ankle injury caused me to hang up the cleats and I replaced all of my free time with baking cookies. Somehow they ended up being something people obsess over and I will forever be grateful for it.
